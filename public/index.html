Fantasy Feuds

Welcome to Fantasy Feuds! This game is currently in pre-beta, so there may be some minor bugs.

Gameplay

Each player is placed on either the orange or blue team. Your goal is to deplete the opposite team's health bar by conquering the most castles

Buying characters

Buy characters by clicking the button. Your gold is shown next to the icon which is the same color as the healthbar above each of your characters.

Add your characters by tapping the map anywhere that is the color of your team. This is either at conquered castles or near characters that are not attacking

Getting Gold

Get more gold by defeating anything with a healthbar.

Move the Camera

Double tap the map to quickly zoom in and out.

Zoom to your characters by pressing or

Zoom and pan around by pinching and dragging (scroll to zoom if you have a mouse).

Control Your Characters

Select your characters by clicking on them or press to select all visible characters, or to deselect all of your characters.

After a character is selected, you can move it by clicking on where you would like it to go on the map. Your phone will vibrate (if your volume is on) if your spot is blocked.

Attacking

Characters will automatically attack anything that is attackable nearby.

Game Over

The team won!
